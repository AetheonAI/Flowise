Stop All Chats

Toggle to activate or deactivate the chatbot. When off, the chatbot won't respond to queries.

Automatic Responses

Allows toggling automatic responses. When on, chatbot responds automatically; when off, queries may be directed to a human.

Profanity Filter

Enable to activate profanity filter, blocking inappropriate language. When off, interaction is more unfiltered.

Learning Mode

Toggle to allow chatbot to learn from interactions for improved performance over time.

Notifications

Toggle to enable or disable notifications.
